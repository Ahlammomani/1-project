MOCKUP-WIREEFRAME COFFEE

Description

My first project involved designing a mockup and wireframe for a coffee shop website. The goal was to create a user-friendly and visually appealing layout that highlights the shop's unique offerings and cozy atmosphere. I focused on structuring the homepage, menu display, and contact section to ensure intuitive navigation and a seamless user experience. This project allowed me to explore design principles, practice wireframing techniques, and develop creative solutions tailored to the coffee shop's brand identity. It was an exciting opportunity to combine creativity and functionality in web design. View Project
